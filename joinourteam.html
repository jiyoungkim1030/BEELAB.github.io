For Prospective Master's Students

The Master’s of Arts in Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) program at Penn State Harrisburg is designed to prepare graduates to develop comprehensive solutions to socially important problems. Inherent in the program study is the course content and practicum experience, a total of 39 credits, required to be eligible to sit for the Behavior Analysis Certification Board Exam. The Association for Behavior Analysis International has verified the courses toward the coursework requirements for eligibility to take the Board Certified Behavior Analyst® examination.
