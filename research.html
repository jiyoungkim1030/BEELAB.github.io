Behavioral Economics and Education

Our lab investigates decision-making and focuses on developing effective procedures to enhance learning for individuals with and without developmental disabilities. We apply behavioral economics concepts to education and other socially significant behaviors and explore the intersection between verbal behavior and decision-making behavior.
